History of the school

Holo Mixed Secondary School was founded in 1981 Sponsored by NOMIYA CHURCH. The School covers an 11 acre piece of land, 1 acre under food production. The School participates in several co-curriculum activities including football, Science and Engineering Fair where the students put out an exemplary performance

Suprisingly The School has aided the aquisition of relevant education to the local community. The School has been consistently improving in academics. Previously the school made an astonishing improvement from a mean of 3.2 in 2022 to a mean of 4.633 in 2023
